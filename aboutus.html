THE LEGEND OF ROBERT

A modern visionary with an old-school recipe.

From One Kitchen to the World

Robert started FFC with a simple philosophy: "Never settle for just good enough." While others focused on speed, Robert focused on the 24-hour marination process that makes our chicken fall off the bone.

Today, Robert's legacy lives on in every bucket we serve. We still hand-bread every piece, following the exact 11 herbs and spices Robert perfected in his basement workshop decades ago.

Our Modern Team

We aren't just employees; we are the guardians of the crunch. Every member of our team is trained in Robert's "Golden Standard" of service.
